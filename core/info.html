The Kaltoran's are a family first species- where even their government is limited in size, and instead communities are largely dictated by major family elders. Part of this connection to family is because the Kaltoran pass down memories through their genes. This confers a lot of advantages, as one would imagine, and is what seems them good at so many tasks.

There are downsides to this kind of information transfering though- after the Great X'ion War the Kaltoran's had to suffer through extreme situations in order to survive.
